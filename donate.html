The Junior Chamber International (JCI) Zamboanga, an affiliate with the Junior Chamber International, is an all-male chapter of your civic citizens in the city of Zamboanga known for its dedication and involvement to worthwhile projects and activities that include community service and care for the environment depicting the roles of catalysts for positive change. Through the efforts of the 2022 Junior Chamber International – Zamboanga Inc. Local Organization President, Junah M. Tamse who answered the call for leadership, and is in the mission of providing development opportunities through several skills-development ventures that will empower young people to create positive change will ultimately make his members a well-rounded leader in managing projects and evaluating impacts that ripples effects across borders.
We are a non-profit organization in need of your assistance in raising funding for our programs. Your contributions will make a significant difference in our community.

JCI Zamboanga Giving Our All for Livability and Sustainability colloquially known as JCIZ G.O.A.L.S. is a flagship program of the Junior Chamber International – Zamboanga; an affiliate of the Junior Chamber International Philippines that is aimed to develop, nurture, advance, and solve the movement of the 17 United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN SDG) before the year 2030.
