JCI Zamboanga Giving Our All for Livability and Sustainability colloquially known as JCIZ G.O.A.L.S. is a flagship program of the Junior Chamber International – Zamboanga; an affiliate of the Junior Chamber International Philippines that is aimed to develop, nurture, advance, and solve the movement of the 17 United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN SDG) before the year 2030. G.O.A.L.S. has its roots down in 2019 when then President, now JCI Senator Jonas B. Delgado has seen the potential of the youth to be agents of change in their community.


The intrepid President furthered the movement by creating projects aligned with the 2030 Agenda whilst preparing next-generation leaders to continue the movement sparked just by an initiative. To this date, the chapter has implemented various projects and programs aligned to combat issues and create sustainable communities that are on par with other budding communities the world is currently molding The problems are too many to approach, hence, with the very limited number of forces working on its accomplishments or realizations, a big chunk of the goals was left unrealized. The emergence of unforeseen events such as the Covid 19 pandemic affects the entire world. We bring the key concepts of UNSDG to the grassroots, Increased number of Zamboagueños in terms of awareness of UNSDG, we don't want to limit the discussion of UNSDG to the academe or government agencies or the organizations whose main focus is UNSDG, but we want to make every Zamboangueños understands the very goals of UNSDG.

The Zamboanga Jaycees is the premier chapter in Mindanao, also became the beacon of positive, sustainable change in the island. An organization who’s dedicated to aiming a positive change has a lot of projects and activities which need fundings. The funds will help sustain the projects and activities. They face problems in keeping society aware of the existence of the organization. In our current project, we propose to have a positive impact on the organization and society.


We will employ a website for a good interaction of society and organization for in-depth information. Hence, along with the current technological innovations, the chapter came up with a project that serves as a platform for the advancement of its mission. JCI and United Nations SDG Action Campaign Renew Partnership to Advance the Sustainable Development Goals The partnership supports the mobilization of young people to take action to create awareness of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and facilitate dialogue with stakeholders around priority areas in their communities. JCI's work with the UN SDG Action Campaign (formerly the UN Millennium Campaign) began in 2004, when JCI committed to advancing the UN Millennium Development Goals (UN MDGs).


Furthermore, through this heightened partnership, both organizations seek to both strengthen the voices of young people in the global arena in creating such solutions and invest in future generations by developing and implementing the next phases of the Global Youth Empowerment Fund’s framework and action plan. About the United Nations SDG Action Campaign: The UN SDG Action Campaign is a special initiative of the UN Secretary-General administered by the UN Development Programme (UNDP) and mandated to support the UN system-wide and the Member States on advocacy and public engagement in the SDG implementation. www.sdgactioncampaign.org
