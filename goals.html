JCIZ G.O.A.L.S.

JCIZ G.O.A.L.S.: Giving Our All for Livability and Sustainability is another flagship project of JCI Zamboanga. Together with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals as its bible, its aim is to create sustainable and impactful projects that will be able to hit all of the goals for communities that it needs to be addressed. In the leadership of JCI Senator Jonas B. Delgado, the chapter is yet to implement more projects and touch thousands of lives for the betterment of society at large.

1 POVERTY

JCI Zamboanga has opened many opportunities for its stakeholders in form of job fairs and alternative forms of income to support their families and slowly move them out of the poverty line.

2 ZERO HUNGER

Through the initiatives of their 2021 President, Project F.E.E.D. otherwise known as Food to Eradicate and End Hunger among the Destitute are giving out food packs to children and people living in the streets.

3 GOOD HEALTH AND WELL-

With medical missions, free medical checkups, blood pressure checkups, and blood testing among others, it ensures its stakeholders are in good shape especially during these trying times.

4 QUALITY EDUCATION

JCI Zamboanga avidly supports this goal by distributing books and school supplies to children in far-flung areas to realize their aspiration to eradicate delinquency amongst themselves.
